FAQs

Q1: How do I choose my location?
To select your location, go to the settings menu and tap on 'Location'. You can then choose from a list of available locations.
Q2: How do I update my profile picture?
To update your profile picture, go to your profile page, tap the edit button, and select a new profile picture from your device's photo library.
Q3: How do I change my profile name?
To change your profile name, go to the settings page, tap on 'Change Profile Name', and enter your new name in the text field. Make sure to save your changes by tapping the 'Save' button.
Q4: How do I change my profile email address?
To change your profile email address, go to the settings page, tap on 'Change Email', and enter your new email address in the text field.
Q5: How do I change my profile password?
To change your profile password, go to the settings page, tap on 'Change Password', and enter your new password. Make sure to save your changes by tapping the 'Save' button.
Q6: How do I delete my account?
To delete your account, go to the settings page, tap on 'Delete Account', and confirm that you want to delete your account by typing 'DELETE' in the confirmation box. Note that this action is irreversible and will permanently delete all of your data associated with this account.

Troubleshooting Guides

App not loading?
Try restarting the app or check if there are any updates available for download. If the issue persists, please contact our support team for assistance.
Profile picture not updating?
Make sure that the image is less than 5MB in size and in a supported format (jpg, png). If you're still having issues, try clearing the app's cache or restarting the app.
